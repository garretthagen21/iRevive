Main Page

This web page is being written as a requirement for the ENGR0011 class at the University of Pittsburgh, but the content in the page is not the position of the university and does not reflect on the university policies. This company does not exist, and this website is for the purpose of teaching students how to program.

You go to pull your phone out of your pocket and suddenly you realize theat you didn't quite have the tight grip you thought you did. You watch in helplessness as your $700 dollar device spirals through the air on to the sidewalk and lands with a devastating thud. With your heart in your stomach you reach down and flip your beloved iPhone over only to face the crushing reality that the screen looks like a jagged spider web. Sound familiar? Breaking your phone is extremely unfortunate, but let's face it, it happens to everyone at some point and there is no need to worry because iRevive is here to help! iRevive is a cell phone repair service that aims to deliver unbeatable quality and customer experience for an affordable price. We offer screen repair, battery replacement, and home/lock button repair on a multitude of devices, including all Apple products. Every repair is backed by a 90 day warranty and we will beat any advertised price by 5 dollars! Headquartered in the 6th floor of the the world-renowned Nordenberg Hall, you can find iRevive's three experienced technicians hard at work. Our technicians can perform repairs within 40 minutes! Our company aims to achieve 100% customer satisfaction on every repair, as we encourage our happy customer's to refer their friends. If there is anything college students are good at, it's having little to no money and shattering their phone screen during a night out. With that in mind, iRevive aims to deliver a service for college students that provides convenient, professional quality repairs for significantly less money than national chains.
